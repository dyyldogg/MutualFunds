Who We Are

Founded by Loyola High School students, Mutual Funds Magazine is a financially independent media organization working to provide continuous news services focused on the leaders within our generation. Our editorial team is composed entirely of highschool students. Mutual Funds Magazine publishes editions at the end of every month.

To submit ideas or concerns to the executive editors, email mutualfundsmagazine@gmail.com.
